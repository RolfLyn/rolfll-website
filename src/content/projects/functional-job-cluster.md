description: 'Job architectures are built top-down and stop being true the moment real work diverges from the plan. You end up with thousands of position titles no one can reason about. I used semantic embeddings and density-based clustering to recover the functional groupings that actually exist in the data, a layer the formal hierarchy did not have. The point was to let the data show the structure instead of imposing one and hoping it holds.'
