I built an AI-assisted discovery layer on top of that data. The model was the straightforward part. The judgment was harder: deciding what is safe to surface and what is not, so the tool answers real staffing questions without becoming a surveillance instrument. That decision became a tiered data-sensitivity framework that governs what the system will and will not show.'
